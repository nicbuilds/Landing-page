Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about carbon credit tokenization

We currently support all major carbon standards including VERRA (VCS), Gold Standard, ACR, and CAR. Our platform is designed to be standard-agnostic, meaning we can adapt to new methodologies as they emerge in the market.
Our tokenization process maintains full compliance with SEMARNAT requirements. Each token represents a verified credit from an approved registry, and we ensure all retirement processes meet Mexican regulatory standards. We work closely with authorities to maintain compliance as regulations evolve.
We primarily use Ethereum and Polygon for their robust smart contract capabilities and environmental efficiency. For enterprise clients, we can also implement private/permissioned blockchain solutions based on specific requirements.
For already certified credits, the technical tokenization process can be completed in 2-4 weeks. If your project needs verification first, the timeline depends on the certification body but typically ranges from 3-6 months. We streamline the process with our pre-approved methodologies and registry relationships.
Costs vary based on project size and complexity, but typically include: verification fees (if needed), smart contract development (one-time), token minting fees (per credit), and a small percentage of transaction value (typically 1-3%). Many clients find the increased liquidity and price discovery offset these costs within the first year.
